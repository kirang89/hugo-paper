## My Photography

Browse through my photography collection hosted on Google Photos.

To embed your own Google Photos album:

1. Open your album in Google Photos
2. Click the share button
3. Get the shareable link
4. Use the shortcode below with your album URL

{{< google-photos "https://photos.app.goo.gl/YOUR_ALBUM_LINK_HERE" >}}

Replace `YOUR_ALBUM_LINK_HERE` with your actual Google Photos album link.
